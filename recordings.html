My Style

I play mostly Rockabilly, Neo Rockabilly and early Rock 'n' Roll from the 1950s. My influences include artists like Elvis Presley, Charlie Feathers and Gene Vincent. I love the raw sound of vintage guitars and tube amplifiers.

These recordings were made with my previous band "The 56 Drivers" and all the songs are covers of classic tunes.
